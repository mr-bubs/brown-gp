Real-Time Analytics

SESSION
TIMING

The Session Timing dashboard dynamically locks onto the current live session, adapting its data feed in real-time. If no cars are on track, it automatically displays the results of the last active session, or provides a complete recap of the previous race weekend.

The data feed adapts automatically to the session type:

  • Races: Track gaps to the leader, pitstop counts, and positions gained or lost. Tap any driver's card to expand and view their complete tyre strategy and stint history.
  • Qualifying: Monitor elimination drop zones and live lap times across Q1, Q2, and Q3 to see the ultimate gaps to pole position.
  • Free Practice: Focus on raw pace by tracking each driver's absolute best lap time and their gap to the fastest car on track.

Historic Analysis

REPLAY
DASHBOARD

The Replay Dashboard acts as your personal F1 time machine. Select any Grand Prix from recent history, and the backend engine will instantly reconstruct the entire race from start to finish using millions of raw telemetry data points.

Take full control of the broadcast with a suite of interactive analysis tools:

  • 3D Track Engine: Experience the circuit like never before. The map renders in a fully interactive 3D isometric view, accurately visualizing real-world elevation changes, steep hills, and bridge overlaps.
  • Interactive Camera: Use the virtual joystick to smoothly rotate and zoom the map, or tap and drag the canvas to focus your camera on specific corners as the cars battle.
  • Dynamic Playback: Watch the race unfold at your own pace. Play, pause, scrub through the timeline, or fast-forward the action at up to 10x speed.
  • Live Gap Tower: Track the entire grid in real-time. The dynamic tower automatically updates driver positions, exact gaps to the leader, and current tyre compounds as the race progresses.
  • Race Control Feed: A dedicated message board displays historic yellow flags, safety car deployments, and driver retirements at the exact second they happened.
